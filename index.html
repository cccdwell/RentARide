ABOUT

In this outrageous comedy, two brothers, Lil Fuk and Dookie, are masters of making something out of nothing—or so they think. Lil Fuk scores a major payday after seducing a passionate nurse into signing off on a bogus lead-poisoning diagnosis, landing a hefty state check. He hands the money to Dookie, challenging him to find a way to flip it into something bigger.

After a humiliating encounter with a racist car rental agency, Dookie comes up with a plan to launch their own business: Rent A Ride, a neighborhood car rental service catering to their community. Business picks up quickly, and one of their first clients is Pastor Lyon, a young, hustling preacher trying to get it.

But when Pastor Lyon mysteriously goes missing, the brothers reclaim the rental car he left behind—only to find a stash of drugs in the trunk. Wanting to make a quick flip, they enlist a small-time drug hustling rapper named Beach Boy, with a golden aura to sell the drugs. The situation takes a dark turn when the Big Boss tracks them down, demanding his missing supply.

Now, with the Big closing in and chaos escalating by the second, the brothers must outwit everyone—racist gatekeepers, street-level hustlers, and a vengeful kingpin—if they want to survive their wild ride to fortune. "Rent A Ride" is a fast-paced comedy about family, hustle, and finding opportunity where no one else dares to look.

Packed with big laughs, unexpected twists, and vibrant characters, "Rent A Ride" is a wild comedic journey about family, ambition, and flipping life's messiest situations into opportunities.
